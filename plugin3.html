When will Pave Planner be available?

We're targeting April 2025 for the official launch. Early access subscribers will be notified first and receive special launch pricing.

Will it work with existing designs?

Yes! Pave Planner works with any surface or curve in Rhino. Simply select your target surface and let the tool do the rest.

What stone sizes are supported?

The tool supports any stone size from 0.5mm to 10mm diameter. You can also mix multiple sizes in a single design.

Absolutely! While we provide preset patterns, you have full control over spacing, orientation, density, and arrangement.
